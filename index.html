About Metaprint

Metaprint is a web application that helps you track your carbon footprint. Our easy-to-use interface allows you to enter your data and calculate your carbon footprint. You can also see how your carbon footprint compares to others and get tips on how to reduce your emissions.

Key Features

Personal Carbon Footprint Tracking

Metaprint allows you to track your daily activities through a set of pre-picked activities or by allowing you to input other activities that contribute to your carbon footprint.

Company Tracking

Metaprint allows you to track the companies you interact with through a set of pre-picked companies or by allowing you to input other activities that contribute to your carbon footprint.

Tips and Tricks

Metaprint allows you to compare your footprint to that of larger companies, while also providing tips and tricks to improve your carbon footprint.

Personal Footprint Overview

Metaprint allows you to view your own data in a daily view, weekly view, or even a range of dates.

Aggregate Footprint Overview

Metaprint allows you to view the aggregate data of all users in a daily view, weekly view, or even a range of dates.
